About Us

We are a Further Education College with two specialist campuses located in Hemel Hempstead and Watford. We believe in transforming life chances and providing access to high quality education for all.

  • West Herts College operates within a framework of policies, procedures and guidance documents to ensure that services are compliant and consistent and enable individuals to participate in activities safely and fairly.
  • We will provide a broad range of provision and services to meet the needs, interests and aspirations of the communities we serve.
  • West Herts College will play a role in transforming life chances

Frequently Asked Questions

We are a Further Education College with two specialist campuses located in Hemel Hempstead and Watford. If you’ve got a question you may find your answer here. If you still need help then contact us on campus, online or by phone.

  • How many days a week am I in college?

    It depends on the course, but it generally works out to be about 3 days. That means you can work part-time or catch up on your studies.

  • What can I do with my free time at college?

    At our campuses you can enjoy our Breakout Spaces, the cafe and the canteen. There's free wifi at all sites, or you can visit the LRC to catch up on your studies.

  • Can I apply for more than one course? 

    If you are undecided you can apply for up to two courses, and attend a Course Introduction Meeting.
